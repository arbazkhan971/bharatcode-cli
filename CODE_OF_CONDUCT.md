# Code of conduct

The BharatCode community is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of contribution.

Expected behavior includes respectful technical disagreement, constructive feedback, empathy,
clear attribution, and a focus on the work rather than the person. Harassment, threats,
discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, sustained disruption, and publication of another
person's private information are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior is
unsafe or persistently disruptive. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented privately,
and free from conflicts of interest. Retaliation against someone who reports a concern is itself a
violation.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).
Include relevant links or screenshots and state whether immediate safety measures are needed.
Maintainers will limit disclosure to people required to investigate and respond.

This policy applies in repository discussions, reviews, project communication spaces, and public
events where someone is representing BharatCode.
